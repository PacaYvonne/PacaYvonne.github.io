Why Professional Testing Matters

Many people live with undiagnosed allergies for years, suffering needlessly because they don't know their specific triggers. Professional testing provides:

  • Accurate identification of all allergens
  • Severity assessment for each trigger
  • Personalized treatment recommendations
  • Access to advanced therapies like immunotherapy
  • Peace of mind and improved quality of life

Common Signs You Need Professional Testing

Respiratory Symptoms

  • Chronic congestion or runny nose
  • Persistent cough or wheezing
  • Frequent sinus infections
  • Shortness of breath

Skin Reactions

  • Unexplained rashes or hives
  • Persistent eczema
  • Itchy or watery eyes
  • Facial swelling

Digestive Issues

  • Stomach pain after eating
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Chronic diarrhea
  • Bloating and gas
