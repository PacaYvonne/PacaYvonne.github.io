I Am A Busy Mom Who Hates Cleaning And These 4 Products Changed Everything

By Sienna Hartley

As a mom, I am constantly juggling a million things - school drop-offs, work calls, meal prep, and the never-ending laundry. Cleaning was always that thing I “meant to do later,” but later rarely came. Then I discovered these five products. They are quick, effective, and so easy that they actually fit into my chaos-filled schedule.

1. The Robot Vacuum That Runs While Life Happens

When you have kids, there’s always something on the floor — crumbs, pet hair, Legos. I used to try sweeping every night, but it never lasted more than a few hours. Now I just set the Bagotte Self-Emptying Robot Vacuum to run during school drop-off. By the time we’re home, the floors look like I actually stayed on top of things. It even dodges toys and gets under the couch. It’s not perfect, but it buys me so much sanity.

2. Pacagen Dust Allergen Neutralizing Spray

Every morning my son would wake up sneezing. At first, I thought it was just seasonal allergies, but it happened even in the middle of winter. Then I learned something that shocked me - the average bed has 1.5 million dust mites, and mattresses can have up to 10 million. I decided to try Pacagen’s Dust Allergen Neutralizing Spray. I started using it every couple of days as part of our morning routine - a few quick sprays on his bedding, our couch, and the rugs. Within a month, his morning sneezing stopped. It was such a small change, but it made a huge difference in how he starts his day. Now I keep it in the bedroom and use it without even thinking. It gives me peace of mind knowing our home’s air is a little cleaner for my kids.

3. Magic Eraser Sponges for Everyday Disasters

Sticky fingerprints on the walls, crayon doodles on the table, mystery scuffs on the baseboards — it’s like they appear overnight. I keep a stash of Magic Eraser sponges in a drawer and reach for them constantly. Just a bit of water and a few swipes, and it all disappears like it was never there. No sprays, no elbow grease. For busy moms, it’s the closest thing to a cleaning shortcut that actually works.

4. Soap-Filled Dish Wand

Our sink used to be a constant pile-up of plates, cups, and snack bowls. Then I bought a dish wand that you fill with soap and leave in the sink. Now, when I or the kids finish a snack, we wash the dish right away. It keeps the sink clear and the kitchen looking less chaotic — and it’s such an easy habit to keep up.

Final Thoughts

None of these products are magic, but together they’ve made my home easier to manage and a little calmer to live in. If I had to pick the one I’m most grateful for, it’s definitely Pacagen’s Dust Allergen Neutralizing Spray. Safe for people and pets, it stopped the sneezing and has been a true game-changer for my family. As a busy mom, that’s all I’m looking for—ways to make the mess feel less overwhelming and the cleaning feel less like a battle.
